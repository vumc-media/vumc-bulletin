Families & Children in Worship

Kid’s Worship

Kid’s Worship is offered during both services. For the first service, families may bring children to Kid’s Worship with Pastor Maddie beginning at 9:15 AM. During the second service, children are dismissed during the announcement time. Parents may pick up children in Room 119 following the service.

Nursery & Sensory Room

Nursery care is available every Sunday for children ages 4 and under in Room 108. The Sensory Room in Room 115 is open to all ages. Children under age 16 must be accompanied by an adult.
